Risk Management for Agile

Risk management in Agile is different from traditional risk management. Traditional risk management is done once at the beginning to help prevent future risks. In Agile, risk management is done at the beginning and continuously throughout the project alongside each sprint. This helps combat risks that come up along the way as well as at the beginning.
