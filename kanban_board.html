What is a Kanban Board?

A Kanban board is a visual tool used to track work items as they move through different stages of the workflow.

Boards can be physical (sticky notes) or digital (Asana, Trello, Jira, Microsoft Planner). The key is visualizing work and limiting work in progress to improve flow.
