Agile Metrics and Reporting

KPIs (Key Performance Indicators) are used to measure the performance of the team and their sprints. Each of these can also be brought up to the top and used in the planning process.

Velocity

+

Measures how much work gets done per sprint. Velocity helps teams forecast future sprints and understand their capacity. Consistent velocity indicates stable team performance.

Predictability

+

Measures how consistently the team can deliver results sprint after sprint. Higher predictability means the team can reliably plan and commit to future work.

Burn Up/Down Charts

+

Track remaining stories in a sprint. Burn Down charts remove completed stories showing work remaining. Burn Up charts add completed stories showing work done. Both visualize progress toward sprint goals.

Cycle Time

+
