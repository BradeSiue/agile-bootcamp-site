Agile project management is a modern approach to planning and delivering work that focuses on collaboration, adaptability, and continuous improvement. Unlike traditional methods, Agile breaks work into smaller cycles, called sprints, allowing teams to adjust quickly, deliver value faster, and maintain better communication.
